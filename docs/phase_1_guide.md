Below is a **practical, reusable Phase-1 system** you can run almost mechanically. It has three parts:

1. Interview script (what you ask, in order)
2. Live mapping template (what you document while talking)
3. Output structure (what you deliver afterward)

No fluff—this is designed so you can run it consistently across SMEs.

---

# 1. PHASE 1 INTERVIEW SCRIPT (45–75 min)

## 🔹 Section 0 — Context (5 min)

Goal: understand what matters before diving into processes

**Questions:**

* What does your company actually do (in simple terms)?
* Where does revenue come from?
* What currently costs you the most time or causes the most frustration?
* If one process magically fixed itself, which one would it be?

👉 You are already filtering for **high-impact areas**

---

## 🔹 Section 1 — Process Discovery (20–30 min)

You now zoom into **one process at a time**

Use this structure repeatedly:

### Core question:

> “Walk me through this step by step as if I had to do it tomorrow.”

---

### You extract:

#### 1. Trigger

* What starts the process?
* (Email? Order? Form? Call?)

#### 2. Steps

* What happens first?
* Then what?
* Then what?

👉 Write each step as a simple line:

```
Customer sends email → Employee reads → Data copied → Tool X → Excel → …
```

---

#### 3. People involved

* Who does each step?

---

#### 4. Tools used

* Where does the work happen?

  * Email
  * Excel
  * CRM
  * ERP
  * Browser tools

---

#### 5. Output

* What is the final result?

  * Invoice
  * Order confirmation
  * Report
  * Updated system

---

## 🔹 Section 2 — Friction Detection (15–20 min)

Now you go back through the same process and apply pressure.

For each step ask:

* Where does this take longer than expected?
* Where do errors happen?
* Where do you copy/paste?
* Where do you wait for someone?
* Where do you check something manually?

👉 This is where **real value appears**

---

## 🔹 Section 3 — Quantification (10–15 min)

Without this, your work stays “nice idea” instead of “decision-ready”.

Ask:

* How often does this happen? (per day/week)
* How long does it take?
* How many people are involved?

👉 Convert to rough numbers:

```
10x/day × 5 min = 50 min/day
≈ 16 hours/month
```

---

## 🔹 Section 4 — Constraints (5 min)

* Are there legal/compliance constraints?
* Can data leave the system?
* Are APIs available or not known?

👉 This prevents unrealistic automation ideas

# 2. LIVE MAPPING TEMPLATE (use during the call)

Use this structure while talking (Notion, paper, or simple doc):

---

## PROCESS NAME:

(e.g. “Invoice handling”)

---

### 🔹 FLOW

```
1. Trigger:
Customer sends invoice via email

2. Steps:
- Email opened
- Invoice downloaded
- Data manually entered into ERP
- File renamed
- Uploaded to folder
- Sent to accounting

3. Output:
Invoice recorded in system
```

---

### 🔹 TOOLS

* Email (Outlook)
* ERP (XYZ)
* Windows folder

---

### 🔹 PEOPLE

* Back office employee

---

### 🔹 FRICTION POINTS

* Manual data entry
* File naming inconsistent
* Double checking required
* Delays when employee absent

---

### 🔹 QUANTIFICATION

* 15 invoices/day
* ~4 min each
  → ~60 min/day

---

### 🔹 AUTOMATION IDEAS (RAW)

* Email parsing
* Data extraction
* Auto-upload to ERP
* Naming standardization

---

👉 Important:
Do NOT refine yet. Just capture.
